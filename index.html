What is Task Manager?

Task Manager is an all-in-one solution for your organizational needs.

What does Task Manager Do?

Easily add and remove your tasks from your task list.

Organize tasks by type, category, or whatever other key words you want so that you can find exactly what you're looking for.

Break larger, long-term goals into smaller pieces that are linked together to easily track progression.
